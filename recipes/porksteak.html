Pork Steak

Pork Steak, or Filipino Bistek, features tender pork slices simmered in a flavorful mix of soy sauce, calamansi juice, and onions.

Ingredients:

    4pcs pork chops
    5 tablespoons soy sauce
    2pcs lime or calamansi
    1/2 cup cooking oil
    2pcs white onions (sliced)
    1 1/2 cups water
    1/2 teaspoon granulated white sugar
    salt and pepper to taste
